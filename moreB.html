Decor Tiles

More Beyond Decor Tiles was created for a tiling business focused on selling of tiles in Ghana

This project began after a representative from More Beyond Decor Tiles saw an advert I shared online. From there discussions regarding the project began and payment was made. The project began with a prototyping phase where the design and features were designed in Figma and upon approval the official website was built. The project took longer than expect but, with patience and professionalsm it came to a fruitful end
